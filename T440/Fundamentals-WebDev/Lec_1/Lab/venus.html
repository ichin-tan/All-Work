Welcome to Venus

Climate

Venus experiences severe greenhouse effects, making it one of the hottest planets in our solar system with surface temperatures stable at around 462 degrees Celsius (864 degrees Fahrenheit). Its dense CO2 atmosphere creates intense heat and pressure that are challenging for typical space gear.

Culture and Customs

While Venus itself does not support life as we know it, our human-made habitats floating above its clouds are a hub for astrobiologists, chemists, and storm chasers. The culture here is heavily centered around research and discovery.

Dining and Cultural Attractions

Try the 'High Cloud' cafe, which offers a zero-gravity dining experience while you observe the turbulent atmosphere below through reinforced viewports. Enjoy a variety of galaxy-famous dishes prepared under extreme pressures.

Unique Attractions

Experience a hover ride through the upper clouds of Venus, where you can witness electric blue waves of light caused by the planet's intense lightning storms—a phenomenon nowhere else to be seen in the Solar System.

Eco-Tourism

Join our efforts to study and preserve the delicate upper atmosphere of Venus. Participate in guided tours to collect samples and learn about our ongoing research to potentially terraform and reduce the planet's surface temperatures.
